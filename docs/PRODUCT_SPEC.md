# Hybrid OS 2.0 Product Spec

## Product principle
Every screen answers one question.

- Today: What should I do today?
- Workout: What am I doing now?
- Progress: Am I improving?
- Program: Where am I in the cycle?
- Profile: What constraints/preferences are active?

## Default training constraints
- Monday-Friday training only by default.
- Saturday/Sunday default to Recovery.
- Rebalancing never pushes workouts into weekends unless explicitly changed later.
- Zone 2 matters as much as lifting.
- Program week advances by execution/manual choice, not calendar drift.
